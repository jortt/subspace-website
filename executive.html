What do the rankings mean?

The ranks are given by the executive council to members of the club. They follow the following guidelines but can also be changed at the will of the current executive council.

Admiral: former president Vice-Admiral: former member that did a great deal of work for the club but was never president Captain: current president Commander: current vice-president LT. Commander: current secretary/treasurer LT: current extended executive LT. Junior: any member who has been in the club for more that a year Ensign: any new member
